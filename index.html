The openSUSE Community

We are a community that provides free and easy access to Free and Open Source Software. We innovate, integrate, polish, document, distribute, maintain and support one of the world's best Linux distributions. We are working together in an open, transparent and friendly manner as part of the worldwide Free and Open Source community.

Join us!

We Write

Open source and openSUSE is not just about developers; it's about contributing to a cause that benefits everyone. Be apart of that cause. Words to describe programs and translations to make these programs known throughout the world are needed. Help us fulfill that need.

Write with us!

We Hack

To produce and develop open-source tools, software, programs and applications, openSUSE counts on developers with different levels of expertise and understanding. Each individual brings a unique set of contributions to the openSUSE community; we value these differences and commend their contributions. openSUSE is not just about furthering the open-source cause, but about improving the collective knowledge, skills and understanding of community members. Contribute to openSUSE's knowledge-based development and "hack with us."

Hack with us!

We Design

There is nothing more gratifying than designing something great; we understand this and live by this mantra. Professional-looking, fun and functional designs are essential for open-source and openSUSE elements to become mainstream. openSUSE welcomes contributions from people who take pleasure in applied arts and seek their contributions and to openSUSE projects. Your audience awaits you.
